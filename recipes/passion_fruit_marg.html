Passion Fruit Margarita

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coarse salt
  • 1 lime wedge
  • 1 1/2 cups ice
  • 5 ounces passion fruit juice
  • 2 fluid ounces silver tequila
  • 3/4 fluid ounces triple sec
  • 3/4 fluid ounces lime juice

Steps

  1. Sprinkle salt onto a plate. Moisten the rim of a glass with lime wedge and press the moistened rim into the salt; fill the glass with ice.
  2. Fill a cocktail shaker half-full with ice. Add passion fruit juice, tequila, triple sec, and lime juice to the shaker. Seal and shake vigorously until outside is frosted, 10 to 15 seconds. Strain mixture into the glass and stir. Garnish with lime wedge.
